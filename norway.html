Norway

Norway is celebrated for its dramatic natural scenery, including fjords, mountains, and the Northern Lights. Oslo, the capital, is known for its vibrant cultural scene and green spaces. Norway is also a leader in sustainable energy, with a significant portion of its electricity coming from hydroelectric power. The country is famous for its Viking history and outdoor activities such as hiking and skiing.
