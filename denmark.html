Denmark

Denmark is known for its rich history and vibrant cultural scene. Copenhagen, the capital, is famous for its historic architecture, canal tours, and as the home of the Tivoli Gardens amusement park. Denmark consistently ranks high in global happiness and quality of life surveys. The country is also noted for its contributions to design and architecture, as well as its bike-friendly cities.
