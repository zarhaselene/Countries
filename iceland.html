Iceland

Iceland is renowned for its otherworldly landscapes, including geysers, volcanoes, and hot springs. Reykjavik, the capital, is known for its lively arts scene and as a gateway to explore the country's natural wonders. Iceland is one of the most geologically active countries in the world and is known for its commitment to renewable energy, particularly geothermal energy. The country is also famous for its unique wildlife, including puffins and whales.
